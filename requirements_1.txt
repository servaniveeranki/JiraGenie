PROJECT: E-Commerce Marketplace Platform
==========================================

Client: ABC Retail Solutions Pvt Ltd
Date: January 2026
Contact: projects@abcretail.com

BUSINESS OBJECTIVE:
Build an online marketplace where multiple sellers can list products and customers can purchase them. Similar to Amazon/Flipkart but focused on Indian market.

TARGET AUDIENCE:
- Customers: Urban millennials, age 25-45
- Sellers: Small to medium businesses, individual vendors
- Geographic: Initially Tier 1 & 2 cities in India

CORE FEATURES NEEDED:

1. Customer Side:
   - Sign up with phone number or email
   - Browse products by category
   - Search and filter products
   - Add to cart and wishlist
   - Checkout with multiple payment options
   - Track orders in real-time
   - Write reviews and ratings

2. Seller Side:
   - Seller registration with business details
   - Upload products with images, pricing, inventory
   - Manage orders (accept, ship, cancel)
   - View sales dashboard
   - Payout tracking

3. Payment Integration:
   - We want to use Razorpay as primary payment gateway
   - Support: Credit/Debit cards, UPI, Net banking, Wallets
   - EMI options for purchases above ₹5,000
   - Cash on Delivery (COD)
   - Automatic refunds for cancellations

4. Admin Panel:
   - Approve/reject seller applications
   - Manage product categories
   - Monitor orders and disputes
   - View revenue analytics
   - Handle customer support tickets

5. Notifications:
   - Email and SMS for order updates
   - Push notifications on mobile app
   - WhatsApp notifications (optional)

TECHNICAL PREFERENCES:
- Mobile app for Android and iOS (React Native)
- Web dashboard for sellers and admin (React.js)
- Backend in Node.js or Python (your choice)
- Database: PostgreSQL or MongoDB
- Cloud: AWS or Google Cloud
- Must handle 10,000+ daily orders

INTEGRATION REQUIREMENTS:
- Razorpay for payments (mandatory)
- Shiprocket or Delhivery for logistics
- SMS gateway (MSG91 or similar)
- Email service (SendGrid)

SECURITY & COMPLIANCE:
- Secure payment processing (PCI compliance)
- User data encryption
- GDPR compliance for data privacy
- GST invoice generation

TIMELINE & BUDGET:
- Phase 1 MVP: 3 months
- Budget: ₹15-20 lakhs
- Team: 4-5 developers

SUCCESS METRICS:
- 5,000 active users in first 3 months
- 500 seller onboarding
- 95%+ payment success rate
- Average order value: ₹1,500

PRIORITIES:
HIGH: Payment integration, Order management, Product catalog
MEDIUM: Reviews, Analytics dashboard
LOW: WhatsApp notifications, Advanced filters

Note: We need the platform to be scalable as we plan to expand to more cities in 6 months.
